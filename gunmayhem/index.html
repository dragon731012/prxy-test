Gun Mayhem 2

What is the Gun Mayhem 2 game?

Gun Mayhem 2 is a fast-paced shooter game developed by Kevin Gu where the goal is to knock your enemies off of the platform and remain the last player standing. The campaign mode consists of 16 progressively challenging levels, and the game can be played either with friends or in single-player against AI.

How to Play

  • Arrow Keys to move
  • Up to jump (press twice to double jump)
  • Down to drop through a platform
  • Z to shoot
  • X to throw dynamite (hold key longer to throw further)
  • Collect crates to obtain better weapons
